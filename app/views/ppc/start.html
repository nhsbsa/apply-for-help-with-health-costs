Prescription Prepayments

You can prepay the NHS for your prescriptions which may make them cheaper.

You can cover all of your prescriptions for 3 or 12 months, no matter how many you need

A prescription costs £8.40 per item, but a prescription prepayment costs:

  • £29.10 for 3 months
  • £104 for 12 months (or 10 instalments of £10.40)

If you are going to buy 4 prescription items or more in the next 3 months, it may be cheaper to buy a prescription prepayment.

Buy a prescription prepayment

To save the NHS money we no longer provide plastic cards.

Before you start

It takes about 5 minutes to complete and you will need your bank or payment card details depending on how you want to pay.

The pharmacy staff will want to make a note of the details of your prepayment. You can choose how we send them to you and use them to start collecting prescriptions straight away.
