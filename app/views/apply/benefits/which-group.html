I'm not sure which group I'm in

The Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) will send you a decision letter to tell you which ESA group you are in.

You will be in either the:

  • work-related activity group, where you’ll have regular interviews with an adviser
  • support group, where you do not have interviews – you’re usually in this group if your illness or disability severely limits what you can do

If you have made a claim for ESA but haven’t been put in a group yet, you will be in the assessment phase.

You are usually in the assessment phase while you wait for your Work Capability Assessment.
