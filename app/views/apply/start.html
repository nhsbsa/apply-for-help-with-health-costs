Apply for help to pay NHS costs

What you could get

You could get help towards your:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports
  • sight tests, glasses and contact lenses
  • travel to receive NHS treatment if you were referred by a GP, hospital doctor, dentist or optician

Your partner and dependant children under 19 will be entitled to the same help as you.

What you need

When you apply, we'll ask for information about:

  • money you get- things like pay, pensions or student loans (your income)
  • your housing costs- things like council tax and rent (your outgoings)
  • any savings or investments you have

If you have a partner, you'll need to tell us about their income and outgoings too.

Documents that could help

You may need your:

  • payslips or latest business accounts (if self employed)
  • student funding notices
  • latest statement of benefits or award notice
  • pension details
  • savings, investments and property details
  • rent or mortgage statements
  • latest council tax bill or council tax benefit letter

Apply now

Other ways to apply

If you can’t apply online, you can apply by post. The form tells you the address to send it to.

We’ll let you know if you can get help within 18 days of receiving your form.
