5. Refund of costs you've already paid

If you pay for treatment before you apply for help, you may be able to claim a refund.

You must claim a refund within 3 months.

Claim a refund for a prescription

You can only claim a refund if you ask for an NHS receipt form FP57 when you pay. The form tells you what to do to claim your refund.

Claim a refund of other health costs

You need to complete a refund form (HC5) and post it back to us.
