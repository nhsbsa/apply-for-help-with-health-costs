Apply for a refund of NHS costs

Use this form to claim a refund of NHS costs you've already paid.


You must claim a refund within 3 months of paying.

Refund forms

England- Refund of NHS dental charges

Ref: HC5(D) PDF, 130KB, 16 pages

England- Refund for sight test, glasses or contact lenses

Ref: HC5(O) PDF, 130KB, 16 pages

England- Refund for the cost of travel

Ref: HC5(T) PDF, 130KB, 26 pages

How to apply

To apply for help with NHS costs by post:

  • print the form for the country you live in
  • fill it in with a pen
  • post it to:
    NHS Business Services Authority
    Bridge House
    152 Pilgrim Street
    Newcastle Upon Tyne
    NE1 6SN

Alternatively, you may be able to get a refund form from Jobcentres, NHS hospitals, GP practices, dentists or opticians.Â
