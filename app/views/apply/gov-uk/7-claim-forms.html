How to apply

To apply for help with NHS costs by post:

  • print the form for the country you live in
  • fill it in with a pen
  • post it to:
    NHS Business Services Authority
    Bridge House
    152 Pilgrim Street
    Newcastle Upon Tyne
    NE1 6SN

Alternatively, you can apply for help with NHS Costs online.
