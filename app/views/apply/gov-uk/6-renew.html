1. How it works

You can apply for help with NHS costs if you don’t already qualify for help and are finding it hard to pay.

You can’t apply if you have more than £16,000 in cash, savings or investments, or £23,250 if you live permanently in a care home.

The amount of help you get depends on your circumstances, but you, your partner and your children under 19 could get help towards the cost of:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental check-ups and treatment
  • sight tests, glasses and contact lenses
  • travel to receive NHS treatment
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports
  • Previous Refund of costs you've paid
