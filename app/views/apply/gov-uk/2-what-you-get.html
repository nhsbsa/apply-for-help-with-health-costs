2. What you'll get

Depending on your circumstances, you could be entitled to either:

  • full help
  • partial help

Any help you get will be valid for between 6 months and 5 years depending on your circumstances.

Warning It’s important that you know when your help expires as you may get a fine if you use it after this date

Full help

If you qualify for full help you’ll get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental check-ups and treatment
  • sight tests
  • NHS wigs
  • fabric supports- things like surgical bras, support tights and spinal support

You'll get money off:

  • new glasses and contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing your glasses and contact lenses
  • travel for NHS treatment

Partial help

If you qualify for partial help you may get help towards the cost of:

  • NHS dental check-ups and treatment
  • sight tests
  • glasses and contact lenses
  • NHS wigs
  • fabric supports- things like surgical bras, support tights and spinal support
  • travel for NHS treatment

You don’t get any help to pay for prescriptions but you could save money by prepaying for prescriptions for a 3 or 12 month period.
