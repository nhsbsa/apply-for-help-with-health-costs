2. What you'll get

Depending on your circumstances, you could be entitled to either:

  • full help
  • partial help

Any help you get will be valid for between 6 months and 5 years depending on your circumstances. It’s important you know when your help expires so you don’t get a fine.

Benefit payments will not be affected by any help you get.

Full help

If you qualify for full help you’ll get:

  • free NHS prescriptions
  • free NHS dental check ups and treatment
  • free NHS sight tests
  • help with the cost of glasses or contact lenses
  • help with the cost of travelling to receive NHS treatment
  • free NHS wigs and fabric supports

Partial help

If you qualify for partial help you’ll get some help towards the cost of:

  • NHS dental check-ups and treatment
  • NHS sight tests
  • travelling to receive NHS treatment
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You may also be entitled to help towards the cost of glasses or contact lenses.

You don’t get any help to pay prescriptions but you can save money on prescriptions by prepaying for a 3 or 12 month period.
