Other ways to apply

If you can’t apply online, you can apply by post. The address to send your application to is at the end of the form.

We’ll normally assess your application form within 18 days of receiving it.

You will need to use the right postage.

You can ask for a form to be posted to you (England and Wales only):

  • England - order a form online
  • Wales – telephone: 0845 603 1108 (select the option for health publications)
