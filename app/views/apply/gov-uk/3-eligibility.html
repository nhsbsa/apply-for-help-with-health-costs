3. Eligibility

Don’t apply if you already qualify for help as you already get full help with your NHS costs.

You won’t qualify for any help if you or your partner have more than:

  • £16,000 in savings, investments or property (not including the place you live)
  • £23,250 in savings, investments or property if you live permanently in a care home (£24, 000 if you live in Wales)

How it’s worked out

The amount of help you get depends on the following:

  • your income (including pensions, investments, benefit payments and student loans)
  • your savings (or does it not matter if it’s under £16k?)
  • property you own (not including the place you live)
  • your housing and council tax costs
  • the people who live with you
  • if you have a disability
  • your partner’s circumstances (if you have one)

If you have less than £128 a week left to spend after you have paid your housing costs and council tax, you are likely to get some help towards your NHS costs.
