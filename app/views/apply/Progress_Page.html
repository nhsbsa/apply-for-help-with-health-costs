What you have to do

  • 1. Confirm your identity

    You'll need to provide personal details, including your name, National Insurance number and date of birth.

    Then you'll need to answer security questions to help us match the details you provide with the information we know.

  • 2. View your tax credits

    Once you've proved your identity you can use the Tax Credits Service to view your next payment and when it will be paid.

    This is a new service. In future, you'll be able to report changes to your work and family life online.

    Currently, you can only report changes using the Tax Credits Helpline. You must tell us about changes during the year so we can make sure you get the payments you're entitled to and avoid overpayments.

  • 3. Provide feedback

    When you're finished, sign out and tell us what you think of this service and help to improve it.
