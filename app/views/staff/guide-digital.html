How to assess a digital application

    • What to do

    1. 1

      View the next digital application available to register
      Click on the green 'next' button to bring up the details you will need to register an application on CRS.

    2. 2

      Register the application on CRS and enter the CRS number into the online system
      Once you've registered the application in CRS, enter the CRS number into the online system and click to blue 'link CRS' button.

      This will make sure that any digital applications can be found using the CRS number if need be.

    3. 3

      View full application details and assess as normal
      View the full application details by clicking on the applicants name.

      You will be able to see each section of information in different tabs, such as income or housing costs.
