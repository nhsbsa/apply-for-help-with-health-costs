Introduction

Applicants may be eligible to pay only part of their fee, depending on their income, whether they have any dependent children or if they are part of a couple.

The exact amount will be worked out for you and a letter generated for you to send to the applicant requesting the part fee payment.

The applicant will have 14 calendar days to provide payment.

Before you start

Check the payment:
  • is for the correct amount
  • is made out to 'HM Courts & Tribunals Service'
  • is dated within 14 days from the date the letter was sent requesting payment

What to do

When correct payment is received:
  • select the application from the list ‘Awaiting payment’
  • log the payment as being received

When incorrect payment is received:
  • try to contact the applicant by phone to request the correct payment
  • if this is unsuccessful, write to the applicant requesting the correct payment
  • For over-payments, return the cheque
  • For under-payments, request a cheque for the additional amount

When no payment is received after 14 calendar days:
  • select the application from the list ‘Awaiting payment’
  • select 'write letter to the applicant' and a letter will be generated for you
  • post the letter and all the documents back to the applicant
