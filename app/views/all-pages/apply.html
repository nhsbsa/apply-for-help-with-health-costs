Apply for help with NHS costs

You can apply for help with NHS costs if you don’t already qualify for help and are finding it hard to pay.

We need to know about any money you and your partner get and also some housing costs, things like council tax and rent.

Documents you may need

Income

  • paylips
  • latest business accounts, if self employed
  • student funding notices
  • latest statement of benefits or award notice
  • details of pensions
  • details of savings, investments and property

Housing costs and council tax

  • details of rent
  • details of mortgage payments
  • latest council tax bill
  • council tax benefit letter

Other ways to apply

If you can’t apply online, you can apply by post.
